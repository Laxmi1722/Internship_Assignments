What is Mass Text?

Mass texting is a communication strategy that involves sending the same SMS message to a large group of recipients simultaneously. While it can reach many people quickly, it often lacks personalization and engagement that modern customers expect. Our solution transforms this approach by enabling personalized, targeted messaging at scale.
